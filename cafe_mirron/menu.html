About Cafe Miron

"Dream, then come to us for the food you dreamt of!" Our journey from the bean to the cup started in the year 2014 when the cafe culture had not yet set its footprint, and coffee was still considered a luxury. Our quest to provide a truly nice experience and brew the perfect one led us to create the largest and most preferred cafe chain in Sri Lanka.
